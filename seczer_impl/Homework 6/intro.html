Bias Mitigation in GPT-based Job Recommendation Chatbots

Digital Humanism Homework 

Objective: Develop strategies for reducing biases concerning sensitive attributes such as gender, age, ethnic background, and country of origin in GPT-based chatbots for job recommendations.

Task: Prepare a report that outlines key measures for mitigating such biases. Take a look at the state-of-the-art, possible directions are:

  1. Data Collection & Processing: Discuss the importance of diverse and representative training datasets for LLMs, and strategies for identifying and mitigating biases in these datasets.
  2. Algorithmic Design: Focus on the unique challenges in designing LLM algorithms, such as handling the complexity of natural language and ensuring the model does not perpetuate or amplify societal biases.
  3. Continuous Monitoring: Emphasize the need for ongoing evaluation specifically tailored to LLM outputs, considering their dynamic and context-dependent nature.
